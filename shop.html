Our story

In 1988, two young people met on an alpine trail.
Aisling had been distracted by an edelweiss and gone a few steps off the track.
Paolo had been distracted by the beautiful lady going off track and had decided that off track was, after all, a very sensible idea.


Fast forward a couple of hours (plus a couple of glasses of génépy) and the two found out they were both fond of nature, hiking and herbs (and of each other too).


She went back to Ireland to pursue her studies in Botany.
He went back to Turin, his hometown, to pursue a degree in Herbal Sciences.
They both found a way to pursue each other around Europe.

The Counter

In 1995, Aisling and Paolo’s passions and studies found their natural
(what else?)
outlet in The Herbalist’s Counter, their shop in Turin’s city centre,
a stone’s throw away from the famous Mole Antonelliana.


The Counter’s offer has kept on expanding since,
slowly but consistently coming to include
a great variety of herbs and teas from around the world.


Some things have remained the same, though:
the two herbalists’ dedication to their work (more of an art, actually) and their customers,
the quality of their products and
the pleasant herbal scent that welcomes those who visit.

Our philosophy

Natural is good.


When there’s a herb there’s a way.


Less medicines, more natural remedies
(whenever possible).


When in doubt, try Nature's way out.


Each person’s needs will vary,
our care for them will not.


Customer satisfaction, product quality and a sound work ethic
make for a great infusion.
